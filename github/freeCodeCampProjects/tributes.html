Chinua Achebe

A literally colossus who revolutionised African literature

Here's a timeline of Chinua Achebe's life:

  • 1930: Born in Ogidi, southeastern Nigeria (now Anambra) on the 16th of January.
  • 1936: Started elementarty school at the St Philips' Central School where he was quickly promoted for due to his higher intelligence.
  • 1942: Moved away from his family to the village of Nekede, four kilometres from Owerri. He enrolled at the Central School where his brother older brother John taught. He was greatly influenced by the Mbari traditional art form during this period.
